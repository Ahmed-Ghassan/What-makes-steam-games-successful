# What make steam games successful
## Introduction:
### What is steam? 
The Steam platform is the world's largest digital distribution service for PC games, owned by Valve Corporation. It acts as a massive online storefront where users buy, download, and manage thousands of games, coupled with a comprehensive social ecosystem that includes friend lists, community forums and steam workshop where players can create their customized maps and games mode. 
### What this analysis about?
As part of this study, we will examine the core characteristics of Steam games, specifically their primary genre, category, and most commonly used languages, and quantify the relationship between these attributes in achieving high rating.
### Scenario
BoogyGames studio has struggled to achieve strong ratings on Steam, with many of their games receiving negative reviews. Since only 32% of Steam games score above 80%, I was brought in to analyze the platform and identify which combinations of Genre, Category,and supported languages are most likely to achieve ratings above 80% positive reviews.
